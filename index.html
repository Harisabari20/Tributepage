=> Kuppusamy Annamalai, popularly known as K. Annamalai, is an Indian politician affiliated with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

=>Current Role: He is the President of the Tamil Nadu BJP.

=>Previous Career: Before entering politics, Annamalai was an IPS (Indian Police Service) officer in the Karnataka cadre, often referred to as the "Singham" of Karnataka.

=>Education: He has a degree in mechanical engineering and an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Lucknow.

=>Political Entry: Annamalai joined the BJP in 2020, citing a desire to serve the nation through politics.

=>Public Speaking: Known for his articulate speeches and sharp criticism of political opponents,he has become a prominent face of BJP in Tamil Nadu.

=>Key Focus Areas: He emphasizes Tamil Nadu's development, youth engagement, and corruption-free governance.

=>Grassroots Outreach: Under his leadership, the Tamil Nadu BJP has intensified efforts to strengthen its grassroots presence in the state.

=>Campaigns and Issues: Annamalai has been vocal on issues like religious harmony, cultural preservation, and economic growth.

=>Vision: He aims to establish the BJP as a major political force in Tamil Nadu, a state traditionally dominated by Dravidian parties.
